About Me

I've always been attracted to both foreign languages and technological innovations. I enjoyed learning and practicing the Spanish, English, German, Latin, Belarusian and Russian languages and literature and in 2012 I got a degree in linguistics. But... I still felt like something was missing. I always wanted to develop a solid skill set as a tech professional. So I signed up for an immersive course at General Assembly, Boston in March 2018.


Even though I had to commute from Cape Cod, where I lived at the time, I am proud to mention that I've never been late, and I managed to stay on track with the studies, mastering my time management skills daily. Obviously, the biggest achievement throughout GA program became obtaining the knowledge and skills of programming languages like JavaScript, Ruby, frameworks like Rails, Ember, Express, understanding the principles of databases, etc. Just within 3 months, I created 4 projects, 3 of which, are full-stack ones.

It is curious to notice that bringing in the linguistic background, helps me to realize language patterns much faster and develop a better awareness of syntactic details of a programming language. Being able to speak multiple languages links to the flexibility to see situations from another perspective, which advances my problem-solving skills as well as lets me be more methodical writing code. At this point I feel super excited to keep writing code, building projects, collaborating and learning to "speak" the language of Web Development.
